About Us

"Law is the backbone of democracy and hope for the human society"

With the purpose of providing legal suggestions, assistance, guidance and counseling to individuals, corporate sector organizations, non-profit organizations, firms and groups, this law firm was established in Kathmandu in 2000 under the dynamic leadership of Mr. Gopal Pandey.

The firm, though registered in Kathmandu valley, expanded its reputation within Nepal and served people from almost all geographical regions in many dimensions. It advocates in all courts of Nepal and mobilizes expert lawyers for quality and prompt service.
